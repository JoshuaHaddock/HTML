Games I Play

Gears Of War

Gears of war is a third person shooter game. It is a unique game as there isn't any other game that is like it.
The first Gears of war game was released in 2006. Gears of war has now got up to 5 games with a Gears 6 in the making. The games are which are:

  1. Gears of war 1
  2. Gears of war 2
  3. Gears of war 3
  4. Gears 4
  5. Gears 5

Gears of war 1-3 was created by a company called Epic Games. However, after Gears of was 3 the rights of the game were sold to Coalition. Coalition were then the ones to develop Gears 4 and 5.

Valorant

Valorant is a first person shooter game. The game released on 2nd June 2020 and now is coming close to it's third year Anniversary. There are many modes on this game and the main ones are:

  1. Unrated - A gamemode where people can play without having to fully try.
  2. Competitive - A rank based mode where people try and compete to try and get a better rank to show off their skills.
  3. Swiftplay - A casual game mode where people can have fun while not having to tryhard.
  4. Deathmatch - A gamemode where you and your team have to gain a certain amount of kills to win before the enemy team.
